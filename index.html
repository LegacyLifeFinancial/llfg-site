Who We Are

A Company Built for
Agent Success

Legacy Life Financial Group is a Field Marketing Organization (FMO) — meaning we sit at the top of the distribution chain, giving our agents access to dozens of the nation's top-rated carriers, the highest available contract levels, and resources that independent agents simply can't access on their own.

As an FMO, we don't just recruit agents — we build them. From licensing support and carrier contracting to ongoing mentorship and lead systems, Legacy Life exists to make every agent on our platform more productive, more profitable, and more independent.

Term Life

Simple, high-demand coverage — easy to sell and essential for families at every income level.

Whole Life

Permanent protection with cash value that grows — a powerful tool for long-term client relationships.

IUL

Indexed Universal Life — tax-advantaged growth tied to market indexes with downside protection. A premium product with strong commissions.

Annuities

Retirement income solutions your clients can count on — and a strong source of recurring commissions for you.

Legal

Privacy Policy & Data Security

Your Privacy Is Protected

Legacy Life Financial Group is committed to maintaining the highest standards of data privacy and security in full compliance with all applicable federal and state regulations, including the Gramm-Leach-Bliley Act (GLBA), the Health Insurance Portability and Accountability Act (HIPAA) where applicable, and applicable state insurance privacy laws. We collect only the information necessary to facilitate insurance transactions, agent onboarding, and organizational operations.

Encryption & Data Security

All agent and client information collected through Legacy Life Financial Group platforms — including personal identification details, financial data, health information, and policy records — is protected using industry-standard 256-bit SSL/TLS encryption. Data is transmitted securely at all times and stored in encrypted environments accessible only to authorized personnel. We employ strict access controls, multi-factor authentication, and regular security audits to ensure the ongoing integrity of all data systems.

Prohibition on Data Sale — Legal Consequences

Legacy Life Financial Group does not sell, rent, lease, or trade any agent or client personal information to third parties under any circumstances. Any unauthorized disclosure, sale, or commercial exploitation of agent or client data — whether by employees, contractors, affiliated agents, or any third party — constitutes a material breach of our terms of engagement and applicable law.

Individuals found to have sold, disclosed without authorization, or otherwise misappropriated agent or client data will be subject to immediate termination of their contract, civil litigation for damages, and referral to the appropriate state and federal regulatory authorities. Violations may result in substantial civil penalties and criminal prosecution under applicable federal and state statutes.

Information We Collect

We collect information provided directly by agents and clients through our secure portal, application forms, and communication channels. This includes name, contact information, professional licensing details, financial information for commission processing, and policy-related data necessary for carrier contracting and client service. We do not collect unnecessary personal information and do not retain data beyond the period required by regulatory and business obligations.

Your Rights

Agents and clients have the right to request access to their personal information, request corrections to inaccurate data, and request deletion of data subject to regulatory retention requirements. To exercise any of these rights, contact our compliance team at [email protected].

This policy is effective as of January 1, 2026 and is reviewed annually to ensure continued compliance with evolving regulatory requirements. Legacy Life Financial Group reserves the right to update this policy with appropriate notice to affected parties. For questions or concerns, contact [email protected].
