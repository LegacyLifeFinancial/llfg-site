Collect and display testimonials, success stories, case studies for the LLFG portal. Auto-request after milestones, carousel display on landing page.

## What to Build
1. Testimonial submission form (name, role, quote, photo, rating)
2. Auto-request trigger after milestones (first deal, $10K month, promotion)
3. Approval workflow for manager review before publishing
4. Landing page testimonial carousel with animation
5. Case study template builder (challenge, solution, results)
6. Social sharing buttons on published stories

## Integration Points
- Social Proof agent (spec_socialproof) manages collection
- Gamification triggers auto-request on milestone
- Landing page renders approved testimonials
