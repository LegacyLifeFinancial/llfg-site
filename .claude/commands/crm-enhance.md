## Premium Standards
- All cards: glassmorphic (rgba background, backdrop-filter, gold border)
- All hovers: translateY lift + gold glow shadow + border color shift
- All transitions: cubic-bezier(0.4, 0, 0.2, 1) timing
- All sections: gold gradient top-line accent
- All values/numbers: Cormorant Garamond serif font, scale on hover
- All tables: hover row highlight, gold accent on active
- All forms: gold focus glow, smooth border transition
- All modals: slide-in with scale animation
- Tab switching: fade-in animation

## Life Insurance CRM Specifics
- Policy tracking: client, carrier, product, premium, status, placed date
- Commission tracking: per-policy earnings with advance/override breakdown
- Carrier status: contracted vs pending per carrier
- Agent pipeline: prospect → contacted → interviewed → approved → contracted → producing
- Team hierarchy: org chart with AP rollup, NOT persistency/chargeback metrics
- Training progress: XP, badges, course completion, certifications
