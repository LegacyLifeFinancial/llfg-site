Build pre-meeting intelligence briefings for the LLFG portal. Pull client history, policy details, renewal dates, talking points, cross-sell opportunities.

## What to Build
1. Meeting prep card that auto-generates before scheduled meetings
2. Client history summary: all interactions, deals, policies
3. Key talking points based on client profile
4. Cross-sell opportunity suggestions based on current coverage gaps
5. Renewal dates and premium amounts at risk
6. One-click brief generation from calendar event

## Integration Points
- Meeting Intel agent (spec_meetingintel) generates briefs
- Calendar Agent provides upcoming meeting data
- CRM contacts provide client history
