Generate and manage content for the LLFG portal — recruiting posts, training descriptions, email campaigns, and agent-facing copy. Uses the portal's existing data to create personalized, on-brand content.

## Before anything else
Read index.html. Find the email templates (`ptab-emailtemplates`), training curriculum (`TRAINING_CURRICULUM`), marketing system, and AI content agent (`LLFG_AIContentAgent`).

## Content Types

### 1. Recruiting Content
Generate for social media, email, and portal:
- LinkedIn recruiting posts (3-pillar: educate, story, engage)
- Instagram/Facebook carousel scripts
- Recruiting email sequences (5-touch: intro, value, social proof, urgency, final)
- Job posting descriptions for insurance advisor roles
- Follow the Digital Marketing course content for tone and strategy

### 2. Training Content Enhancement
- Course descriptions for the Training tab cards
- Module intro summaries
- Unit learning objectives
- Quiz questions for each unit (for future quiz feature)
- Completion celebration messages

### 3. Email Campaign Copy
- Drip sequences for new recruits (onboarding nurture)
- Client follow-up templates (7-touch retention protocol)
- Referral request templates
- Policy review invitation sequences
- Birthday/anniversary client touchpoints

### 4. Agent-Facing Portal Copy
- Dashboard welcome messages (role-specific)
- Achievement notifications (badge earned, level up, course complete)
- Motivational quotes (for QOTD rotation)
- Tool tips and help text for portal features

## Brand Voice Guidelines
- Professional but approachable
- Confident, not aggressive
- Educational, not salesy
- Use "we" and "your" (not "I")
- Short paragraphs, active voice
- LLFG brand colors: gold (#c9a84c), cream (#f5f0e8), black (#0a0a0a)
- Fonts: Raleway (body), Cormorant Garamond (headings), Cinzel (brand)

## Content Generation Workflow
1. Identify content type and audience
2. Pull relevant data from portal (team stats, training progress, etc.)
3. Generate draft following brand voice
4. Output in ready-to-paste format (HTML for portal, plain text for email/social)

## Integration Points
- AI Content Agent: can generate images via Pollinations.ai
- Email Agent: content feeds into email campaigns
- Marketing Agent: content feeds into marketing automation
- Training Coach Agent: content enhances training recommendations

Read index.html before making any changes. Use portal data to personalize content.
